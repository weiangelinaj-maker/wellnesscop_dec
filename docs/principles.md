* 
**We Practice Radical Accountability:** We believe trust is built when we listen to needs and demonstrate commitment to our promises.


* 
**We Belong to Each Other:** We operate under the belief that "we are all in this together," creating an authentic space for genuine connection.


* 
**We Prioritize Healing:** We are committed to reducing systemic trauma by embedding prevention and holistic wellbeing into our planning.


* **We Reclaim Our Joy:** We value the meaning in our work. We support each other in finding "Compassion Satisfaction" so we can serve without depleting ourselves.


* 
**We Follow Community Wisdom:** We lift up community voices to ensure our decisions are grounded in lived experience.


* 
**We Share Power:** We move away from silos and toward collaboration, recognizing that we are stronger when we correct power imbalances.
