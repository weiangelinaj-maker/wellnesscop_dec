### **Part 4: The Ecosystem (Systemic Advocacy Frameworks)**

Goal: Addressing the root causes of trauma (poverty, racism) through external partnership.

#### **For Funders & Philanthropy**

* 
**Unrestricted Funding:** Push for long-term, unrestricted grants that allow for agility.


* 
**Lived Experience:** Prioritize funding for organizations led by People With Lived Experience (PWLE).


* 
**Systems Storytelling:** Fund narratives of collective action, not just individual success stories.



#### **For Government & Policymakers**

* 
**Doughnut Economics:** Advocate for frameworks valuing wellbeing (health, happiness) over pure GDP growth.


* 
**Trauma-Informed Legislation:** Advocate for laws addressing Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs) and prevention.


* 
**Transformative Justice:** Move systems away from retributive models toward community healing.



#### **For Medical & Health Systems**

* 
**Social Determinants:** Treat the whole person by addressing housing and poverty, not just medical symptoms.


* 
**Universal Screening:** Implement valid trauma screening for youth and families.
